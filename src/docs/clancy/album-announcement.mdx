## 02/28/2024 New single 'Overcompensate' is announced

On 28th of February, the band [tweeted out a teaser](https://x.com/twentyonepilots/status/1762901004264108391?s=20) to their upcoming single, Overcompesate.
The single premiered the next day, February 29th, at 1PM EST.

## 02/29/2024 New album announced

Prior to the release of the new single, the band's website updated to tease the new album.
The album, Clancy, was revealed to come out on May 24. 

The album cover and tracklist can be seen on the CD's and vinyls in the band's stores.
